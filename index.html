Experience

I am an advanced developer with more than 10 years of professional experience.

I have created websites for clients such as Agency.com, Tribal DDB, Tequila and Saatchi Interactive on websites for LG, Budweiser, The Carbon Trust, Toshiba and others. For Newangle I created a number of interactive touchscreens for museums. I write clean, well organised code and have built applications using a variety of frameworks.
